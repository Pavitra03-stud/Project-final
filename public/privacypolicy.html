Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Rooba's Catering collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you register, make a booking, contact us, or use our services. This may include:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone, address)
  • Booking details (event date, number of guests, menu choices)
  • Account information (login credentials, profile data)
  • Payment information — processed via our payment provider (we don’t store full card details on our servers)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for purposes including:

  • Providing and personalizing our services and bookings
  • Processing payments and communicating about orders and reservations
  • Responding to your inquiries and providing customer support
  • Sending administrative notices and promotional communications (you may opt out)
  • Improving and securing our website and services

3. Cookies & Tracking

We and our partners use cookies and similar technologies to provide essential site functionality, remember your preferences, and analyze site usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling cookies may affect some site features.

4. Third-Party Services

We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (payment processors, hosting providers, analytics). These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only to provide their services to us.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. If you suspect a breach, contact us immediately (contact details below).

6. Children

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us to request deletion.

7. Your Rights

You may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can usually manage your information through your account settings or by contacting us. We will respond to requests in accordance with applicable law.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy here with the "Last updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated where required by law.
